- "When when accepting user-supplied text in new subcommands..."

But it reveals something about the abstraction pipeline: it was designed to strip *content* (specifics that identify a particular repo), not to produce *prose* (text that reads well). The pipeline is a machine, and machines don't proofread. The hive will fill over time with entries that begin "When when" — a tiny scar that marks the boundary between automated promotion and human curation.

🧒 **in plain terms:**
