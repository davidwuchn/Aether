What is Aether?

An operating system for AI agents

Aether adds a persistent, learning colony layer on top of AI coding assistants. Instead of isolated sessions, your AI agents share memory, coordinate work, and get smarter with every phase they complete.

🧠
Persistent Memory
Colony state, decisions, and learnings persist across sessions. Workers pick up exactly where the last one left off.
⚡
Parallel Workers
Builder, Watcher, Scout, and 21 other specialist agents work in coordinated waves — each focused on their caste.
📡
Pheromone Signals
Guide every worker with FOCUS, REDIRECT, and FEEDBACK signals. Hard constraints and gentle nudges, all in plain English.
🔬
Quality Gates
Built-in security scanning, coverage analysis, and performance measurement happen automatically at every phase boundary.
🌐
Hive Brain
Wisdom learned in one project propagates across all your colonies. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.
🚀
Autopilot Mode
/ant-run chains build, verify, and advance automatically — pausing only when your input genuinely matters.
How It Works

From idea to shipped, in phases

Aether breaks every project into phases. Each phase is executed by a coordinated wave of AI agents, verified, and committed to colony memory.

# 1. Initialise your colony
$ aether init "Build payment flow"

# 2. Plan phases automatically
$ aether plan

# 3. Build and advance
$ aether build 1

# Workers build, test, and verify
# each phase — then advance
